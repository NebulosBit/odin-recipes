Spaghetti Carbonara

Get ready to indulge guilt-free with our vegan twist on the classic Italian dish - Spaghetti Carbonara. Made with smoky plant-based bacon, creamy cashew sauce, and perfectly cooked pasta, this dish proves that you don't need animal products to enjoy a decadent meal. So go ahead, take a bite and savor the deliciousness!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 6 ounces pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Pecorino cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add pancetta or bacon and cook until crisp.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan cheese, Pecorino cheese, minced garlic, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta or bacon and toss to combine.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat and let it cool for a minute. Then, add the egg mixture to the skillet and toss quickly to coat the spaghetti.
  6. If the sauce is too thick, add reserved pasta water a little at a time to thin it out.
  7. Serve the spaghetti carbonara hot, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and black pepper. Enjoy!
